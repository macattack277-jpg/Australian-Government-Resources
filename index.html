Official Government Websites

Access primary Australian government portals and key federal departments for citizens and residents.

  • Australia.gov.au - The official gateway to Australian government information and services, providing access to all federal, state, and territory resources.
  • Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) - Australia's national statistical agency providing comprehensive data on the economy, population, demographics, and society.
  • Australian Taxation Office (ATO) - Official tax and superannuation information, lodgment services, and financial compliance resources for individuals and businesses.
  • Services Australia - Access to Centrelink payments, Medicare services, Child Support, and other social security benefits and programs.

Health & Medical Services

Australian health department resources, Medicare information, and public health programs.

  • Department of Health - Official health policies, Medicare information, public health initiatives, pandemic response, and healthcare program details.

Education & Training Resources

Educational policies, funding programs, and training initiatives across Australia.

  • Department of Education - School education policies, higher education resources, vocational training, student support programs, and funding information.

Police Forces - National & Regional

Law enforcement agencies across all Australian states, territories, and federal jurisdictions.

  • Australian Federal Police (AFP) - National federal police agency responsible for federal law enforcement, counter-terrorism, border protection, and international policing operations.
  • NSW Police Force - New South Wales police service covering metropolitan Sydney and regional NSW areas.
  • Victoria Police - Victoria state police service covering Melbourne and regional Victoria.
  • Queensland Police Service - Queensland state police service covering Brisbane and regional Queensland areas.
  • South Australia Police (SAPOL) - South Australia police service covering Adelaide and regional South Australia.
  • Western Australia Police Force - Western Australia police service covering Perth and regional WA.
  • Tasmania Police - Tasmania state police service covering Hobart, Launceston, and regional Tasmania.
  • Northern Territory Police, Fire and Emergency Services - Northern Territory police service covering Darwin and remote NT communities.
  • Australian Capital Territory Policing - Australian Capital Territory police service covering Canberra and ACT region.

Police Support & Specialist Bodies

Specialized law enforcement agencies and crime prevention organizations supporting Australian communities.

  • Australian Criminal Intelligence Commission (ACIC) - National criminal intelligence agency combating serious and organized crime across Australia.
  • Crime Stoppers Australia - Anonymous crime reporting service enabling community members to report criminal activity safely and confidentially.
