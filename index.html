Official Government Websites

Access primary Australian government portals and key federal departments for citizens and residents.

  • Australia.gov.au - Provides access to MyGov login and other government services.
  • Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) - Provides data on Australia's economy, population environment and more. Useful for research.
  • Australian Taxation Office (ATO) - Everything to do with your taxes.
  • Services Australia - Access to essential services such as Centrelink, Medicare, and Child Support.

Health & Medical Services

  • Department of Health - Official health policies, Medicare information, public health initiatives, pandemic response, and healthcare program details.

Education & Training Resources

  • Department of Education - School education policies, higher education resources, vocational training, student support programs, and funding information.

Police Forces

Every state/territory has it's own police force, including the whole of Australia

  • Australian Federal Police (AFP) - The police force for the whole country.
  • NSW Police Force - The police force for New South Wales.
  • Victoria Police - The police force for Victoria.
  • Queensland Police Service - The police force for Queensland.
  • South Australia Police (SAPOL) - The police force for South Australia.
  • Western Australia Police Force - The police force for Western Australia.
  • Tasmania Police - The police force for Tasmania.
  • Northern Territory Police, Fire and Emergency Services - Northern Territory police service as well as fire and emergency services.
  • Australian Capital Territory Policing - Australian Capital Territory police service covering Canberra and ACT region.

Police Support & Specialist Bodies

Specialized law enforcement agencies dedicated to specific areas of crime prevention and security.

  • Australian Criminal Intelligence Commission (ACIC) - National criminal intelligence agency dedicated to combatting serious and organized crime across Australia.
  • Crime Stoppers Australia - Allows members of the community to report criminal activities discretely.
